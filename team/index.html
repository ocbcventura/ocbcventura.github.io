Our Team
 

DARRYL RATULANGI

DARRYL RATULANGI

Managing Director

As the Head of OCBC Ventura, Darryl is focused on making the OCBC Ventura vision a reality by articulating and driving our strategic roadmap as well as enabling the launch and growth of new ventures and investments into Indonesia digital ecosystem. He drives our culture of innovation and also leads a team of passionate investment professionals that are driven to grow companies from the ground up.

 

Darryl is an experienced M&A professional with a track record of leading and closing deals in Indonesia. He is skilled in creating growth equity story, company restructuring, financial analysis with deep knowledge of Indonesian consumer, and technology space.

 

Prior to joining OCBC Indonesia, Darryl was the M&A Director for an investment banking firm in Indonesia specializing in mid-cap companies in the Consumer (F&B, retail, manufacturing, tech) and Healthcare sectors.

 

Darryl holds Bachelor of Commerce degree in Accounting and Finance from University of Toronto.

ANDREAS ARDHIANTO

ANDREAS ARDHIANTO

Director

Andreas is an experienced finance professional with passion in deals execution and corporate finance. He will work closely with the venture founders and partners to help them discover new business opportunities by leveraging on the OCBC vast network of clients, assisting them to design the proper finance and accounting work-stream to support the venture’s growth, and provide advice on the financial strategy for the next phase of growth.

 

Andreas previously worked at PwC Advisory Indonesia where he managed a team of professional consultants in advising on infrastructure projects for public and private sector. His clients include State-Owned Enterprises, infrastructure bank, and multilateral donors such as the World Bank. He helped clients by providing advice on commercial strategy and optimum financial structure to deliver the project. Prior to joining PwC Advisory, Andreas was in an investment bank where he helped on the deals execution and fund raising for clients ranging from consumer, healthcare, and shipping companies.

Andreas has undergraduate degree from Diponegoro University in Accounting and Finance.

NADIA ALEXANDRA

NADIA ALEXANDRA

Investment Professional

As part of the investment team at OCBC Ventura, Nadia handles deal sourcing, investment execution, and portfolio management.

 

To support the growth of OCBC Ventura and its portfolio companies, Nadia leverages her prior experience, which includes accounting, consulting, and product management. She previously worked with the design, marketing, and engineering team at Traveloka to discover new business models, attract new market segments, and boost the merchandising and upselling efforts for flight product. Prior to Traveloka, Nadia was a Management Consulting Analyst at Accenture, where she worked on strategy development and implementation for State-Owned Enterprises and conglomerate companies.

 

Nadia graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles with a Business Economics major and an Accounting minor.

DYAH TRISNAWATY

DYAH TRISNAWATY

Investment Professional

As part of the Investment team at OCBC Ventura, Dyah sources new deals, executes investments, manages portfolio and actively participates in startup events.

Prior to joining OCBC Ventura, Dyah was a management consultant at Accenture, specializing in finance and enterprise performance. Her clients included State-Owned Enterprises, Government Bodies, conglomerate companies, and private banks, where she and her team helped developing and implementing the companies’ growth strategy, mainly through digital finance transformation. With her long experience in strategy and consulting, Dyah will support OCBC Ventura to grow and expand their portfolio.

Dyah holds Bachelor of Commerce degree in Accounting and Finance from University of Melbourne and Universitas Indonesia.
