About Us
 

OCBC Ventura is a venture capital arm of PT Bank OCBC NISP Tbk, part of OCBC group. We are looking to back entrepreneurs in becoming the change-makers in Indonesia’s digital ecosystem. Our goal is to leverage the group extensive network, financial expertise, customers reach, and combine it with the agility and out-of the box idea from startup to create endless possibilities.
